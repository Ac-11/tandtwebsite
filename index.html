About Us

Welcome to Amritsar

Swagat Tour and Travel, founded by Rohit Arora after a personally challenging trip planning experience, is dedicated to transforming how people explore the vibrant city of Amritsar and its surroundings. We specialize in crafting seamless, enjoyable, and stress-free travel experiences tailored to your unique needs.

Our comprehensive services include customized trip planning, holiday destination exploration, and taxi rentals, all designed to prioritize your comfort and safety. At Swagat Tour and Travel, we take the hassle out of your journey, ensuring you can fully immerse yourself in creating unforgettable memories while we handle every detail.
